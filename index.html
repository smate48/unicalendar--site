Import Help

UniCalendar works with timetable files exported from Neptun in .ics format. Export the file from Neptun, then open it with UniCalendar.

Recommended Import Flow

  • Export your timetable from Neptun as an .ics file.
  • Open the file with UniCalendar from the file-opening option.
  • After import, set the reference day that counts as A week.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

UniCalendar is designed to help students manage timetables exported from Neptun. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app uses and how that data is handled.

1. Data Storage

UniCalendar stores timetable-related data locally on your device. This includes imported timetable files, manual class entries, settings, widget data, and A/B week configuration.

2. Account Registration

UniCalendar does not require account creation, login, or profile registration.

3. Remote Services

UniCalendar does not require a remote backend for its core timetable features. The app is intended to work locally on your device.

4. Data Collection

UniCalendar does not sell your personal data. The app is not designed to collect personal timetable data for advertising or third-party profiling purposes.

5. Support Contact

If you contact us by email or through Discord, we may receive the information you choose to include in your message, such as your email address, device details, screenshots, or bug descriptions.

6. Third-Party Services

If you choose to use external links such as email or Discord, those services operate under their own privacy terms and policies.

7. Your Control

Because UniCalendar primarily stores timetable data locally, you can remove most stored app data by deleting timetable entries in the app or uninstalling the application from your device.
